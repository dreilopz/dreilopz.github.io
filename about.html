About this website¶

In my case, maintaining a personal website is one of those duties that grows out of necessity. So here I am claiming a portion of the web/internet/whatever. Hopefully it is useful/educational for someone/something, that’s the main idea. But there are other reasons. It also helps with: improving my writing skills, making available ways of contacting me, making efforts to improve the quality of published material [if I think about other reasons I’ll write them here]...

This site is created with Sphinx and is light on graphics. You are encouraged to send general commentaries, complaints, broken links reports, corrections, and so on to m (AT) dreilopz (DOT) com. This site has been active since november 2011 and is permanently under construction.
